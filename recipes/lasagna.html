Lasagna

    this is some of the best lasagna you will ever make
  • meat: ground beef and sausage
  • onions: yellow and white
  • sugar: two tablespoons of white sugar for the sauce
  • spice and seasonings: this will be storebought seasoning with dried basil leaves and salt
  • lasagna noodles: storebought or homemade
  • cheeses: parmesean, mozzarella, and ricotta cheeses
  • eggs: to help bind the riccotta
  1. make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. make the ricotta mixture
  4. layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. let the lasagna rest before baking
when your done layer the ingredients in the following order
  1. noodles
  2. ricotta
  3. meat
  4. cheese
